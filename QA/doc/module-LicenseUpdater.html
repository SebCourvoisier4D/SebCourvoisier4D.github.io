Module: LicenseUpdater

The LicenseUpdater module is dedicated to the update of 4D licenses. This module requires the modules https://nodejs.org/api/fs.html, https://nodejs.org/api/path.html, https://nodejs.org/api/os.html, https://github.com/jprichardson/node-fs-extra and https://github.com/codexar/npm-extfs
