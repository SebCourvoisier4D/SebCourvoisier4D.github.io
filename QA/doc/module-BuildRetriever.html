Module: BuildRetriever

The BuildRetriever module is dedicated to the download of the builds to test (from http://vip.download.4d.com/), according to their availability (as published by TeamCity). This module requires the modules https://nodejs.org/api/fs.html, https://nodejs.org/api/path.html, https://nodejs.org/api/os.html, https://github.com/caolan/async, https://github.com/isaacs/node-glob, https://github.com/request/request, https://github.com/sergi/jsftp, https://github.com/thejoshwolfe/yauzl, https://github.com/npm/node-tar and https://nodejs.org/api/zlib.html
